How to play?
  • Click on "Play" button to start the game.
  • Notice the coloured boxes. It will get hightlight.
  • After all sequence of box hightlighting get finish you will have to click the boxes as it was highlighting to select.
  • After select click on the "Submit" button to submit your answer.
  • After completing successfully each level you can get promote to next level
  • If you unable to submit the current answer, the game will reset to level 1.
